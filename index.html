About Me:

Hello! My name is Ernie Enriquez. I am an Economist with the Bureau of Economic Analysis. I hold a B.Sc in Economics from the University of DePaul. I am passionate about the intersection of data, technology and free and open-source software (FOSS). I have prevously worked with the National Economic Council at the White House during the Obama Administration, the International Trade Administration at the US Department of Commerce, and the Chicago Housing Authority.
